Semantics and Digital Preservation: Basic Concepts, Theories and Trends

Description

Part 1 takes a glance at the problem of meaning, and its connection via information to digital preservation.

  • Understand the difference between sentence and word meaning, and how semantic decay endangers access to digital objects over time.

  • Understand the shared concern of philosophy, semantics, semiotics, logic, and information science.
